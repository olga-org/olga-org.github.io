# International Startup Founders Finland (ISFF) and Business Helsinki Community Meeting

*Published on 16-02-2026, 1 min read*

> “Community built by founders, for founders”

That was the slogan of the first, now already historic International Startup Founders Finland (ISFF) and Business Helsinki community meeting.

And no, this wasn’t just another organizational get-together. The session had a very practical ambition: to help founders bring more structure and clarity into startup life by understanding how the neuroscience explains the work of the brain, and how that knowledge can support better planning, prioritization, and teamwork.

![ISFF Community Meeting](/assets/Publications/isff1.jpg)

A big thank you to:

-   **Loan (Cindy) Tran** for the introduction, insights, and energy,
-   **Marina Berzina** for knowledge sharing on neuroscience fundamentals,
-   **Anna Antonenko** for reminding us of the importance of planning and prioritization,
-   and **Sherry Oso** for the creation of a friendly and open atmosphere.

We left not only with new tools but also inspired by the conversations, the networking, and the ideas already forming for future collaboration.

A strong start for a community built by founders, for founders.
