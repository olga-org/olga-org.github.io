# [My Arctic15: A Day of Discoveries and Connections](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/my-arctic15-day-discoveries-connections-olga-kryuchkova-s5cff)

*Published on 2024-06-08 08:33*

Attending [Arctic15](https://www.linkedin.com/company/arctic15events/) was nothing short of extraordinary, packed with productivity and rich in discoveries. I attended the event for the first time and was so impressed that you'll have to excuse my enthusiastic praise. I almost wish I could have stretched it out beyond the single day I was able to attend, given so much value was packed into such a short time.

First and foremost, a hearty thanks to the organizers [Arctic15](https://www.linkedin.com/company/arctic15events/) for orchestrating an event that was not only intimate and friendly but also exceptionally comfortable. Everything was compactly organized: from the stage for presentations and pitches to the startup stands.

![Photos by OlgaK](/assets/Publications/arc1524.jpg)

I was particularly impressed by the spacious areas dedicated to the main focus: networking meetings. These could be scheduled quickly and without delays through the Deal Room application.

![07.06.2024](/assets/Publications/arc15241.jpg)

The entire team, including volunteers, executed the event with the highest level of professionalism and efficiency, keeping a supreme standard for mass gatherings. Now, while [Slush](https://www.linkedin.com/company/slush/) is great (I mean, who doesn’t love a little grandiosity?), [Arctic15](https://www.linkedin.com/company/arctic15events/) has officially claimed the top spot in my startup event rankings.
