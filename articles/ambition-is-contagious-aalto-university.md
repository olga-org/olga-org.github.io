# [Connecting with the Innovation Ecosystem at Aalto University](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/olgakruchkova_the-best-event-in-helsinki-today-happened-activity-7444827126920433665-Cw28)

*Published on 04-04-2026, 2 min read*

The best event in Helsinki today happened at Aalto University, a place where everyone passionate about technology and innovation could truly connet with the ecosystem at Startup Sauna and make the first touch with Aalto Startup Center.

And as I write this, the legendary mixes by Alexander Warén are still playing in my head. His music has that rare ability to warm the atmosphere at any event and bring people closer together.

The main program was short, yet incredibly powerful. A huge thank you to Aape Pohjavirta for a truly outstanding presentation! One idea that stayed with me is the drive to desire and dare for more, to aim not just for incremental progress, but for projects as bold as exploring other planets. That level of ambition is what fuels real innovation and inspiring me 😊. Aape's speech genuinely made my day! Because the truth is: we NEED ambitious projects. They shape ecosystems, push boundaries, and challenge all of us to grow, evolve, and reach new heights 💓.

And beyond the talks, there was the simple joy of being surrounded by curious minds: students, founders, builders. Reconnecting with familiar faces, discovering new perspectives. A special highlight was meeting Sini Karhunen and talking about space food in action in Aalto campus 😄! It added a fun and memorable twist to our whole experience.

**My wisdom takeaway:**
Surround yourself with people who think bigger than you. Ambition is contagious especially in the right environment, it becomes the force that transforms ideas into realities.
