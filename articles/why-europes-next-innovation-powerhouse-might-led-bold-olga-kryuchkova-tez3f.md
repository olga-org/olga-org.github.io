# [Why Europe’s Next Innovation Powerhouse Might Be Led by This Bold Voice from Helsinki](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/why-europes-next-innovation-powerhouse-might-led-bold-olga-kryuchkova-tez3f)

*Published on 23-07-2025, 10 min read*

*Today, we have the pleasure of speaking with* ***Roméo Rinne****, a dynamic force in Finland’s innovation and entrepreneurship landscape. As* ***Co-Founder of Venue Lab*** *and* ***Nexuses****, and the* ***Executive Director of Xes Helsinki****, Roméo’s work sits at the intersection of technology, community building, and visionary leadership.*

*At* ***Venue Lab****, Roméo with the team is dedicated to empowering individuals and businesses to thrive within the dynamic world of live music. They state their mission as creation a more inclusive and vibrant ecosystem where grassroots musicians and local venues can connect, grow, and flourish. By bridging gaps in the live entertainment landscape, they aim to usher in a new era of accessible, sustainable, and community-driven musical experiences*. *Meanwhile,* ***Nexuses*** *is aiming to supercharge the Entrepreneurship Society (ES) ecosystem by providing the support needed to build world-class startup communities.*

*In his role at* ***Xes Helsinki****, Roméo leads a vibrant startup community that acts as a catalyst for early-stage founders, providing resources, peer support, and a launchpad for ideas that push boundaries. His ability to move fluidly between grassroots ecosystems and strategic foresight makes him a rare connector of people, ideas, and futures.*

*In this conversation, we explore Roméo’s perspective on building meaningful networks, scaling impact-driven ventures, and the future of founder support in Finland, Europe and beyond*.

**IV. Tech, AI, Data & Ethics**

[**Olga K.:**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/olgakruchkova?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAAATJhgEBZ_w0JhIORwWZzJQhbMEFPgD75Vk) Where have you intentionally embraced a tech trade-off—choosing speed over transparency or data without perfect consent—and how did you justify it?

[**Roméo:**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rom%C3%A9o?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAADJyV7kBorANu7xi_M7JTVgQMer7YUj1rOI) The only tech trade off we have embraced at Venue-Lab is building slower than we would have wanted to. We have a certain standard and those ethics we were not willing to trade off. I can’t say I regret it, we have a vision, and we are sticking to it! Building slower has allowed us to be extremely cost-effective with 100% ownership and a software that is continuously being upgraded

Olga: Where has Finnish tech/AI culture imparted blind spots or biases in your worldview, and how are you building counterbalances into your systems?

[**Roméo:**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rom%C3%A9o?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAADJyV7kBorANu7xi_M7JTVgQMer7YUj1rOI) Perhaps a general answer, but customer reviews. As a founder you want all your customers to benefit and enjoy using your service. Feedback is the number 1 way to improve, however it is still something as a founder that weighs on your mind.

[**Olga K.:**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/olgakruchkova?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAAATJhgEBZ_w0JhIORwWZzJQhbMEFPgD75Vk) How are you designing tech not just for scalability but for supporting vulnerable, fragile communities whose voices are often drowned in mainstream innovation?

[**Roméo:**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rom%C3%A9o?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAADJyV7kBorANu7xi_M7JTVgQMer7YUj1rOI) I think Venue-Lab is a perfect example for this. At Venue-Lab we help venues manage their finances and save time and money booking live musicians. But at the same time, our team is committed to supporting grass-root live musicians. We don’t charge a monthly fee or a commission from artists and we intend to keep it this way. Our niche is live musicians and by giving them a platform and a voice we believe we can give them opportunities not seen before.
