My Arctic15: A Day of Discoveries and Connections

Created on 2024-06-07 14:36

Published on 2024-06-08 08:33

Attending Arctic15 was nothing short of extraordinary, packed with productivity and rich in discoveries. I attended the event for the first time and was so impressed that you'll have to excuse my enthusiastic praise. I almost wish I could have stretched it out beyond the single day I was able to attend, given so much value was packed into such a short time.

First and foremost, a hearty thanks to the organizers Arctic15 for orchestrating an event that was not only intimate and friendly but also exceptionally comfortable. Everything was compactly organized: from the stage for presentations and pitches to the startup stands.

I was particularly impressed by the spacious areas dedicated to the main focus: networking meetings. These could be scheduled quickly and without delays through the Deal Room application.

The entire team, including volunteers, executed the event with the highest level of professionalism and efficiency, keeping a supreme standard for mass gatherings. Now, while Slush is great (I mean, who doesn’t love a little grandiosity?), Arctic15 has officially claimed the top spot in my startup event rankings.

A highlight was the opportunity to reconnect with familiar faces, engage in forward-looking discussions, and of course huge thanks for the new connections with Anssi Uimonen, Jerri Laine, Magnus Hambleton, Tuomas Pahlman, Dr. Lucas Mittelmeier, Mads Viborg Jørgensen, Riikka Rajaviita, Anton Piippo, Nuutti Räisänen, Katariina Komulainen, Henry Goodwin, Miikka Miettinen, Lauri Karjalainen, Johanna Fräki, Martin Ericsson, Vedat Öndas, Juho Risku, Jouni Mäkelä, Mikko Pitkänen,  Celine Schulze, Markus Räikkönen, Tessy Antony-de Nassau and many others.

My gratitude to everyone who made the event so memorable. I'm looking forward to seeing you all at future events, where we can continue this exciting journey of innovation, mutual support and partnership!

P.S. I completely forgot to mention: the food was top-notch, especially for a mass event. With a wide selection of main dishes, drinks, desserts, and fruits, it was all designed to keep the energy of startups high. Honestly, I was so impressed I almost tried to pitch my startup idea at the dessert frige!
